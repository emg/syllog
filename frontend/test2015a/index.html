Welcome to Syllog (version 2015a)

Here you must decide, whether a syllogism, randomly generated by the system,is valid or invalid. The syllogism will appear when you click the leftmost button named 'New syllogism'.

Then you must decide whether the syllogism is valid or invalid, and indicate your decision by using the buttons named 'Valid' and 'Invalid'.

The particular type of validity which must be used, is the Aristotelian one (and not the modern one that follows from use of Venn diagrams).

This means that all the concepts used in the syllogisms are assumed to correspond to non-empty sets.
